Who are we?

We are Stream, a digital movement redefining how Africans experience and earn from entertainment. Built on the belief that your time online should be valuable, Stream connects people, creativity, and opportunity in one continuous flow.

Through partnerships with artists, record labels, movie studios, and content distributors, Stream serves as a bridge between creators and the audience. We help them reach real streamers and in return, we collaborate on digital campaigns. Users are rewarded for completing specific engagement tasks, streaming media, and verifying content.

Stream transforms ordinary digital engagement into tangible skills and opportunities. Every time you collaborate, you're not just partnering, you're participating in a gig economy where creativity and productivity flow together.
